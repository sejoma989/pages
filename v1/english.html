About me

Hi, I'm Jose, I want to thank you for being here. My interest in creating web elements began in 2017 while I was finishing my professional degree. The challenge of developing a complete wedding planning system and simulating a production environment with several teams co-creating with each other led me to learn and work with HTML, CSS, JS, and agile methodologies. Thanks for coming here. I want to share with you my attraction to web development.
